Registration for JoSAA 2025

Registration and choice filling for JoSAA 2025 will end on 12th June 2025 at 5PM. You will NOT be able to make any changes to your choice list after this deadline. If you do NOT lock your choices, then your last saved choices will be locked automatically at 5PM on 12th June 2025..

If you wish to register for JoSAA, or if you wish to modify your choice list, please do so before 5pm on 12th June 2025. Please note that registering and choice filling in JOSAA 2025 is mandatory for all qualified candidates to obtain a seat in an IIT, allocated through JOSAA 2025.
